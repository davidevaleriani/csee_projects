Your goal is to find the expected value of winning a poker hand given certain pocket cards (i.e. "hand") and a certain amount of players in the table. Data is self-explanatory, but in a more intuitive way:

n_players → Total number of players (ranges from 2 to 10)
pocket_0 → The first card you hold
pocket_1 → The second card you hold
board_0 → The first board card (part of the flop)
board_1 → The second board card (part of the flop)
board_2 → The third board card (part of the flop)
board_3 → The fourth board card (part of the turn)
board_4 → The fifth board card (part of the river)

Cards have the following interpretation:

Training

20000 trials representing combinations of 7 cards. Each trial is associated to a label representing the value of the hand.

Testing

20000 trials representing combinations of 7 cards. A subset of these is used to compute the ranking during the submission, while the entire set will be used for computing the final ranking.
